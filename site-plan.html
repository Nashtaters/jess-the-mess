Overview

Purpose

My company makes crafts by hand and by order. Is there something that you want made, or need more decorations for your home?
That is what our website is all about. We aim to have it clear and have contact info for the orders and price range very easy to see.
There won’t be any need for a lot of info, or useless jargon, just the information that is most important to the customers.

Audience

The audience is young women ages 18-30 years old. If there is any young woman that is interested in making crafts or have cute
decorations for their home, then this is the place to look. For women that want to have handmade crafts, this is the place for them.
All of our crafts are handmade and unique to each order
***orders of 10 or more must call ahead***

Content

Home page

Why Come Here?
There are many people that need to have arts and crafts. Young people ready to head
off to college, young moms getting the new nursery ready for their baby, grandmothers
wanting to get the perfect gift for their grandchildren, to friends that want to give their
friends the best wedding gift ever. All of these people can come to this website and get
all of that right here at Jess The Mess. Here you will be able to pick out a certain craft
design that you want. It can range from something very small and insignificant to the big
and bold and something that will make the neighbors envy you and wonder where you got
such cute ideas!

Images for the Home page

Orders Gallery

Browsing
There can be a wide variety of questions that will be answered just by exploring this
site. If you are wondering what can be ordered, how to order, or need a selection to
choose from, look and ask no further. We got that covered for you! If you are worried
about the cost and the price of everything, that is totally answered as well. We will give
you a price estimate before you are sure of what you want. We will also give you all of the
color options that we have and if you just want us to make you something with a simple
description, we will be more than happy to do that as well.

What to Order
These projects are made by hand by an individual. This means that individualized orders can
be made. If someone wants a project done a little differently than the ones pictured this can be
accommodated because the items are made after they are ordered and so the changes can be
done. There will be more options available to our customers than any other crafting site and
we try to build a better relationship with our customers. They are more than just a number
and a price tag. They are our friends and people that share our love of creativity and
imagination. We have the tools and ability to make products that are professional and
will last for years to come unlike products you may buy elsewhere, such as big brand
companies. Each item is made carefully and with love. We treat each craft as if it will
be presented in our own home and take great care to make it the best it can be. Our
products are unique and cannot be found elsewhere. On the second page of our site
we have a great selection of pictures of things that we are able to craft for you!
These are not all of what we can craft, but just some examples that could help get more
ideas of what you might want.

Images for the Page 2

Contact Us

How to Order
The ways to order through this site are guaranteed to be very easy and simple to use. We are aiming to
fulfil peoples dreams of stickers, vinyl cutouts, to woodworking and customized décor. We
will have a simple contact us page that has our customers enter their name, email and a brief
(or lengthy) description of they want. Our goal is to let these customers leave feeling very
satisfied and confident that their ideas and imaginations will come to life before their eyes
and we want to promote imagination and creativity to everyone. We want to do our best to bring
these things to life!

Information
It is important for us to give the customer all of the information that they want. For example,
we want to be upfront of prices and how long each craft or order may take. We also want
to have the selection of certain options that we have available. We also want to make sure
that everyone is aware that all of the crafts are made by hand and all are unique and not
manufactured in a factory. We also want to inform how long shipping may take or if
pickup is an available option. That would be discussed after an order has been made.
